A bit about me...

My career did not begin in software and web development, in fact I started out as a project manager! Despite enjoying that space and its challenges, I always found myself gravitating towards IT projects involving the implementation of software and websites.

4 years into my career, I decided to switch to what I am truly passionate about... and here I am!


As a developper

I am currently completing a Bootcamp in full-stack web development. In addition to using html, css and javascript to create a front-end website or webapp, I can also integrate third party APIs for additional functionalities. Checkout my portfolio to see some of my work.

Building on the front-end knowldge, I am now learning the principles of back-end development using nodejs. Soon I will be able to build a full stack application.

Outside of the Bootcamp, I have developped desktop software using Java, a language that I have been using for the last two years for various solutions.

As a project manager

Having successfully completed the Prince2 Practitioner and Prosci certifications, I possess the necessary knowldge to implement large-scale projects.

Managing multiple software implementation projects has helped me gain valuable experience, and allowed me to understand both the business side and the technical side.

I believe that my Project Management knowledge and experience give me an edge as a developper. They allow me to see the big picture, and help me deal with obstacles and issues in a productive approach that enables the project to reach its objectives.
